SAVE CHANDUBI

A citizens' appeal to stop illegal sand mining in Kolohi (Kulsi) river and Chandubi Lake, Assam. The serene Chandubi Lake, formed after an earthquake in 1897, is a vital biodiversity hotspot and a source of income for communities. Urgent action is needed to prevent irreversible environmental damage.
